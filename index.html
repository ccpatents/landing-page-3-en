‘‘Too many patent search volume?’’

You can reduce the search volume by specifying search ranges such as abstract or claims.

Entering synonyms can broaden your search results.

But Connecting synonyms or specifying search ranges is a pretty tiring task.

CCPatents solved this problems simply.

‘‘Let’s choose the search range.’’

The contents of a patent document are implied in Title > Abstract > Claims fields order.

The Title field may be considered as a category of a patent, and the conponents or purpose of a patent may be included in the Abstract field.

You can reduce your search volume while increasing accuracy just by specifying the words to be searched for specific field.

‘‘Let’s find hidden patents.’’

There are many different terms used in each patent document.

'flexible' can be expressed as 'foldable' in some documents and 'bendable' in some documents.

Please, Compare your search results of "flexible display" and "foldable display" from Google Patents.

Perhaps the search results are different. So You have to link synonyms with the OR operator and synonym sets with the AND operator.

Step2. Generating a search string

When you press the magnifying glass button, you will see windows where you can enter synonyms for each keyword.

CCPatents has Wordnets built-in and you can enable in the settings, but they are not recommended because of the high noise.

However, the words you add (orange tags) are automatically saved, and then automatically retrieved (blue tags).

And you can specify search ranges for each keyword.

Please choose from All(entire document), Title(category), Abstract(components, purpose) or Claims(contents protected by law)

Finally, select a patent search site and click the Generate button to create a search string.

USPTO is a patent search service provided by the US Patent Office, and Google Patents is a service that applies Google search engine to patent DB.

Step3. Searching patents and repeat

When you click the Search button, the search sting is sent to the site you selected, and the results are immediately displayed.

If there are too many results or irrelevant patents (noises), you can easily narrow the results by changing the search ranges or deleting unnecessary words.
